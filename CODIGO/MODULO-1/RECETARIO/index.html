Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep
5 mins

Cook
5 mins

Total
10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 Large eggs
    room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs and milk until lihgt and frothy.
  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.
  • Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
  • Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward.
  • When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half.
  • Slide the omelette onto a plate.

Nutrition Facts

  • Carbohydrates: 1g
  • Protein: 13g
  • Fat: 26g
